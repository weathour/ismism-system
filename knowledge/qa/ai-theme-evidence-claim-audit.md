## Audit rule

A synthesis claim is accepted only when it links to at least one of:

- an `ev:ai:*` exact quote record;
- an AI W3 draft sense;
- an AI W5 draft relation;
- a W10 card id;
- an explicit row id already represented in the AI manifest.
