# Urban / Housing / Migration / Space Taxonomy

Status: G026 evidence taxonomy. Counts are derived from `urban-housing-migration-space-row-manifest.jsonl`.

## Classes

- `urban-public-space`: city/metropolis/public-space evidence where spatial order appears as an everyday social carrier.
- `housing-dwelling-property`: house, buying-house, dwelling, residence, or home-place evidence; not a housing-market claim unless the row says so.
- `rural-urban-difference`: city/rural/乡村/城乡/土地 evidence where spatial life becomes uneven development, agriculture, or rural-urban transition.
- `migration-drifting-mobility`: migration, drifting, movement, or dislocated residence evidence.
- `spatial-stratification`: space plus class, hierarchy, capital, production, or institutional ordering.
- `spatial-formalization-bridge`: philosophical or formal spatial language that can bridge to the spatial matrix but is not by itself urban/housing evidence.
- `urban-capital-infrastructure-bridge`: urban/land/public-space evidence crossing political economy, organization, infrastructure, governance, or production.
- `excluded-keyword-only`: boundary-control rows whose spatial term is too generic or thin for direct Urban / Housing / Migration / Space claims.

## Boundary decisions

- No exact G026 clean-text hit was found for `住房`, `租房`, `房价`, or `户籍`; those labels require indirect evidence and must remain downstream query labels.
- `空间` and `空间化` often function as ontology/phenomenology/matrix vocabulary. They enter the theme only when paired with public, city, house, land, rural, class, production, institution, or subject-position evidence.
- `家` was not used as an evidence keyword because it overmatches author names, schools, and generic “people/experts”; dwelling claims require 房子、买房、居住、居所 or similar exact support.
- external material or external urban-studies concepts may only seed questions; final claims must trace to row/segment/quote evidence.
