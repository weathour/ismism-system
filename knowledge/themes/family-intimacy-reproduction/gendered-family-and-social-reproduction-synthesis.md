# Gendered Family and Social Reproduction Synthesis

- status: G010 focused synthesis
- scope: 妇女 / 儿童 / 家务 / 父权 / 夫权 / 族权 / 社会再生产

## Reading

The strongest Family evidence is not the generic presence of “women” or “children”; it is the claim that economic liberation, women’s liberation, and children’s liberation must be read together. Row 330 explicitly makes economic, women, and children’s emancipation one process. Rows 327, 321, 325, 331, 335, 337, and 341 connect household dependence, patriarchal authority, reproduction, body, labor, and future generations.

Diagnostic grammar:

`domestic order → woman/child/parent subject-position → household and reproduction mechanism → patriarchal/familial fantasy → family/economy carrier → dependence/inequality contradiction → emancipation or reorganization opening`.

## Evidence markers

- `ev:family:0330:*` anchors the triple liberation of economy, women, and children and the critique of husband/father/clan authority.
- `ev:family:0327:*` anchors women/family liberation inside production-activity emancipation.
- `ev:family:0321:*` anchors household work, ideal society, and family organization.
- `ev:family:0363:*` anchors regeneration, kinship, and intergenerational hope as a broader reproduction problem.

## Use

Use this synthesis to connect everyday family pressures to social reproduction without reducing them to private blame. The valid move is from exact row evidence to mechanism; the invalid move is to turn the layer into unsupported claims about all families or current policy.
