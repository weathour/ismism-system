# Psychological Distress / Anxiety / Addiction / Social Symptom Taxonomy
- status: draft taxonomy for G023 evidence package.
- boundary: corpus diagnostic layer, not clinical/therapy/current-events authority.

## T01. anxiety-depression-distress

anxiety, depression, pain, fear, despair, or explicitly named psychic distress.

## T02. nihilism-flatness-cynicism

nihilism, flatness, cynicism, lying-flat, boredom, or loss of meaning as social symptom.

## T03. addiction-enjoyment-compulsion

addictive enjoyment, compulsion, desire loop, pleasure capture, or drive structure.

## T04. symptom-pathology-medicalization

symptom, disease, treatment, cure, madness, neurosis, pathology, and medicalized naming.

## T05. repression-trauma-psychic-wound

repression, inhibition, trauma, wound, and psychic blockage.

## T06. private-psychologization

social contradiction translated into individual psychology, counseling, self-management, or private repair.

## T07. social-contradiction-private-suffering

bridge rows where social contradiction and individual suffering meet.

## T08. desire-subjectivity-psychoanalysis-bridge

bridge rows to psychoanalysis, subjectivity, fantasy, Big Other, enjoyment, and unconscious desire.

## T09. excluded-keyword-only

false-positive or boundary rows where a keyword is present but social-symptom diagnosis is not licensed.
