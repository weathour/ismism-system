## Core diagnostic

The education theme treats education not as neutral school policy but as a social mechanism: examination, credential sorting, school discipline, academic hierarchy, expert authority, and knowledge production jointly produce subject positions. The reusable grammar is:

`education/exam phenomenon → student or knowledge-worker subject-position → credential/discipline mechanism → merit or expert fantasy → school/academic carrier → contradiction → learning/practice opening`.

Evidence anchors include `ev:edu:0002:01` for 应试教育, `ev:edu:0076:02` for 升学/文凭, `ev:edu:0284:01` for 灌输, and `ev:edu:0334:02` for 纪律教育.

## Diagnostic payoffs

1. **Credential sorting**: rows such as 2, 58, 60, 76, 94, 133, 210, and 231 show education as sorting and recognition, not merely learning.
2. **Knowledge discipline**: rows 277, 283, 284, 285, and 286 make 灌输 / 学习 / 写作 visible as mechanisms of theoretical formation.
3. **Expert authority**: rows 76, 80, 86, 177, 277, and 293 bind academic hierarchy to knowledge-worker authority and cultural hegemony.
4. **Practice opening**: rows 285–287 prevent the theme from becoming anti-education cynicism; learning, research, writing, and exchange become possible practice routes when their institutional mediation is visible.

## Boundary
