# Knowledge Discipline and Expert Authority Synthesis

- status: G007 focused synthesis

The second axis of the education theme is **knowledge discipline**. Rows 98, 174, 277, 283, 284, 285, 286, 293, 334, and 360 show that education is also a procedure for turning knowledge into discipline, authority, organization, or action.

`灌输` is not used as a slogan here. In row 284, the evidence bank fixes it as a row-local mechanism: theory is simplified, transmitted, and made usable for action. Rows 285–287 then make learning/research/writing/交流 into practice routes rather than neutral self-improvement.

Expert authority is the adjacent mechanism. Rows 76, 80, 86, 177, 277, and 293 show how experts, universities, academic systems, papers, and knowledge workers can either stabilize authority or become a site of contradiction. The theme therefore supports a diagnostic question: when does knowledge open practice, and when does it become credentialed obedience?
