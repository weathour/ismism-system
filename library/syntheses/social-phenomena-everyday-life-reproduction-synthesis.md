## Core question

How do labor, credentialing, family, and consumption reproduce everyday subjectivity?

This phase answers: everyday subjectivity is reproduced when the subject is repeatedly placed in four linked scenes:

```text
work / performance scene
→ exam / credential scene
→ family / intimacy / reproduction scene
→ commodity / desire / lifestyle scene
→ repeatable self-relation: I must prove, price, justify, improve, love, consume, and reproduce myself.
```

The synthesis should not be read as a universal sociology of modern life. It is a repository-local diagnostic route: a way to move from concrete prompts such as `内卷`, `考公热`, `婚恋市场`, `消费主义`, or `打工人` into the ISMISM evidence layer.

## Diagnostic chain

### 1. Labor gives the subject a priceable and replaceable form

The Labor layer does not reduce work to employment. It names the mechanism by which work, output, ranking, discipline, and production relation make the subject legible as a managed capacity.

Evidence route:

- theme synthesis: `library/themes/labor-workplace-precarity/labor-workplace-precarity-synthesis.md`
- focused synthesis: `library/themes/labor-workplace-precarity/work-discipline-and-involution-synthesis.md`
- classes: `work-discipline-metrics`, `labor-alienation-subjectivation`, `production-relations-labor-process`, `precarity-career-anxiety`
- concept handles: `指标等级制`, `绩效定价`, `自我商品化`, `劳动者物化`, `失业焦虑`, `不稳定生活`
- relation route: `指标等级制 → 绩效定价 → 自我商品化 → 劳动者物化`

Diagnostic use: a query such as `内卷`, `绩效`, `加班`, or `打工人` should first ask how the person is made comparable, rankable, priceable, and replaceable. The valid move is not moral complaint about competition; it is the row-backed move from metric discipline to subject-position.

The Education layer names the mechanism by which examination, credential, academic hierarchy, and expert authority convert learning and knowledge into a social sorting apparatus.

The failure mode is to treat credentialism as individual vanity rather than an institutional carrier of sorting, family expectation, expert legitimacy, and class reproduction.

## Cross-theme mechanism map

| Link | Diagnostic movement | Evidence-routed interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| labor → education | performance metrics seek credential guarantees | Work ranking and exam ranking share a form: the subject is compared through externally legible measures. |
| education → family | credential promise becomes family investment and intergenerational pressure | Family expectation can turn learning into reproduction of social position rather than free inquiry. |
| family → consumption | household/intimacy demand goods, signs, hope, and lifestyle | The family scene can carry commodity signs of success, safety, love, and future. |
| consumption → labor | enjoyment and identity require income, credit, performance, and market participation | The consumer subject returns to work as the condition for purchasable identity. |
| education → consumption | credentials become self-brand and exchangeable prestige | Recognition can appear as a marketable qualification, not merely knowledge. |
| labor → family | precarity becomes private anxiety and household obligation | Work instability is often lived as family burden, marriage pressure, birth delay, or care crisis. |

## Practical diagnostic protocol

For a concrete Route 1 query, use this route:

1. Start with the relevant query helper and manifest row.
2. Inspect two or more exact evidence records from the evidence bank.
3. Identify the subject-position: worker, student, parent/child/partner, consumer, or hybrid.
4. Name the mechanism: metric discipline, credential sorting, social reproduction, commodity fetishism, market enjoyment.
5. Check concept draft handles and relation draft relations.
6. Use close-reading cards only as close-reading aids; close-reading remains `pilot-draft`.
7. Write the diagnosis as a mechanism, not as moral blame or current-events commentary.

## Boundary and failure modes

- Do not claim that every work pressure is `内卷`, every exam is credentialism, every family relation is oppression, or every purchase is commodity fetishism.
- Do not import news, policy, demographic, labor-market, school-system, or platform facts unless a later project explicitly builds an external evidence layer.
- Do not upgrade concept/relation/close-reading status; concept/relation remain `draft`, close-reading remains `pilot-draft`.
- Do not erase the productive or practice dimension: education can be learning, family can be care and regeneration, work can be organized practice, and consumption can disclose desire; the diagnostic task is to identify when these are overcoded by sorting, discipline, reproduction, and commodity fantasy.
