## Four-field movement

- Field 1 stabilizes scientific reality through paradigm, community, crisis and revolution rather than through an unmediated external object.[row 11, term:范式:s02, position 1-1-2-2]
- Field 2 destabilizes static ontology by tracking eventality, time, space, equality and mechanism transformations.[row 120, term:事件性:s01, position 2-2-1]
- Field 3 turns symbolic systems, subjectivity and dialectic into contested structures such as constellation, Event, site and atonality.[row 229, term:星丛:s01, position 3-2-4-2]
- Field 4 reconnects theory to research, communication, feasibility and practical transformation.[row 285, term:理论现实化:s01, position 4-1-2-1]

## Movement links

- The system moves from scientific paradigm training to metaphysical mechanism when stable knowledge encounters historical or conceptual crisis.[row 11, term:危机:s01, position 1-1-2-2]
- The system moves from metaphysical mechanism to idealist structure when ontology becomes a problem of symbolic position, site or constellation.[row 263, term:site:s01, position 3-4-2-2]
- The system moves from idealist structure to praxis when theory must be tested against reality and rendered usable for action.[row 285, term:现实理论化:s02, position 4-1-2-1]
