# Academic Community, Papers, Metrics, and Research Subjectivation

This synthesis covers how the corpus links academic community, expert identity, paper/metric evaluation, and research subjectivation.

## Mechanism

- Row 2 connects expert formation with market/community valuation: it names the “有知识的专家,” the expert community market, and “论文数量等——指标等级制” (`ev:sar:0002:02`; row 2, `ev:sar:0002:03`; row 2).
- Row 76 shows academic-community rules limiting the analysis of science and experts: “完全信任理工科专家” and “学术共同体内部的一些规矩” are not treated as mere education credentials but as expert-subject positions (`ev:sar:0076:01`; row 76, `ev:sar:0076:03`; row 76).
- Row 292 gives a counter-form: a work-week/reading-group academic community not simply identical with bourgeois academic hierarchy (`ev:sar:0292:01`; row 292, `ev:sar:0292:02`; row 292).

## Boundary against Education

Education owns exams, credentials, classroom discipline, and student sorting. This synthesis owns research valuation, expert/STEM subjectivation, academic-community reproduction, and paper/metric mechanisms.
