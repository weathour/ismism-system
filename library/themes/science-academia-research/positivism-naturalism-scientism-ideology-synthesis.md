# Positivism, Naturalism, Scientism as Ideology

The theme treats scientism, naturalism, positivism, and logical positivism as corpus-internal ideological forms: they claim scientific warrant while carrying ontological, epistemological, and community commitments.

Use this synthesis for `科学主义`, `实证主义`, `自然主义`, `科学化`, and `逻辑实证主义` only with direct evidence. It is not an external history of analytic philosophy or sociology.
