## Boundary rules

- `production-relations-institutional-form` requires production activity/relation evidence plus organizational or institutional consequences; generic mentions of production are insufficient.
- `social-reproduction-family-life` requires family/care/gender/population/life-maintenance evidence or a donor theme bridge; generic `孩子` or `女性` hits remain boundary-only.
- `state-bureaucracy-legitimacy` requires legal, bureaucratic, procedural, command, legitimacy, or administrative function; generic `国家` references are not enough.
- `class-structure-mobility` requires structure-position, mobility blockage, wealth/poverty, class, or humiliation mechanisms; generic success talk is bridge-only.
- `spatial-infrastructure-migration` requires city/rural/housing/migration/space/infrastructure mediation.
- `ideology-culture-education-reproduction` is for institutionalized reproduction through knowledge, schooling, culture, discourse, and expert authority.
- `finance-capital-abstraction-deagency` is not a generic money class; it requires financial abstraction, proxy mediation, credit, capital abstraction, or de-agency.
- `practice-socialization-transformation` requires a practical reorganization opening; it is not a utopian endpoint.
