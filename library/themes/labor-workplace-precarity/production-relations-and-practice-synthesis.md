# Production Relations / Class / Practice Synthesis

- status: evidence-marker synthesis
- theme: labor-workplace-precarity

This synthesis records the pathway from labor as production relation to labor as practice opening.

## Main route

`生产关系劳动场 → 生产过程组织 → 劳动客体化 → 阶级劳动矛盾 → 生产再政治化 → 劳动实践开口`

The route prevents two reductions: first, reducing work to individual psychology; second, reducing production to neutral technique. In the Labor layer, production can be depoliticized through technical or productivity language, but it can also be re-politicized when class contradiction, organization, and collective practice become visible.

## Bridges

- Capitalism: production relations, exploitation, commodity form.
- Education: credential/work channel and academic metric labor.
- Feminism/social reproduction:生活资料、家庭、妇女儿童与劳动再生产.
- Time-Death: labor time pressure and finite life.
