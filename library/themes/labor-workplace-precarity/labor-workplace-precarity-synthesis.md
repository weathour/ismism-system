# Labor / Workplace / Precarity Synthesis

- status: evidence-marker synthesis for G004 closure
- protocol: `library/protocols/social-phenomena-diagnostic-protocol.md`

## Core claim

ISMISM does not treat labor as a neutral job category. In the Labor / Workplace / Precarity layer, labor becomes a social-diagnostic hinge where subject-position, production relation, metric discipline, class contradiction, social reproduction, and practice organization meet.

## Diagnostic grammar

Phenomenon: 打工、工作纪律、劳动异化、失业焦虑、指标化评价。
Subject-position: 劳动者把自身理解为可定价、可排名、可替换的对象.
Mechanism: production relation and metric discipline turn work into a managed social field.
Fantasy/misrecognition: productivity, merit, credential, or market value appears as personal worth.
Carrier: workplace, school-to-work channel, academic metric, market, family/reproduction, class organization.
Contradiction: the worker is asked to be both free subject and priced object.
Practical opening: trace the mechanism back to production relation, organization, and collective practice rather than private failure.
