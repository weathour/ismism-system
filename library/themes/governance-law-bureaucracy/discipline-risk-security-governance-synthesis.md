# Discipline / Risk / Security / Governance Technology Synthesis

- status: G019 focused synthesis
- evidence markers: discipline-obedience-command, governance-control-management, risk-security-management, institution-organization-administration

## Thesis

The governance-technology axis diagnoses the passage from organization to command, discipline, management, and security. It captures how subjects are made governable: by being organized, educated, measured, secured, ordered, and made responsible for the continuation of a procedure or collective project.

## Use

Use this synthesis for everyday institutional experiences: discipline education, organizational obedience, risk/security justification, administrative control, and management as a substitute for transformation. Keep all claims anchored to evidence bank IDs.
