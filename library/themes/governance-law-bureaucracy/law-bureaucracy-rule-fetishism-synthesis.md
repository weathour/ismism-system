# Law / Bureaucracy / Rule Fetishism Synthesis

- status: G019 focused synthesis
- evidence markers: law-rights-legalism, bureaucracy-kafka-judgment, rules-order-normativity, language-game-rule-bridge

## Thesis

The law-bureaucracy axis diagnoses how legal form, rule, procedure, and bureaucratic mediation can detach from practice. In the strongest rows, law and rule do not merely constrain action; they create a scene in which responsibility is deferred to procedure, judgment becomes opaque, and obedience appears as rational order.

## Use

Use this synthesis for phenomena such as rule-as-justice, paperwork legitimacy, Kafka-like procedure, legalistic evasion, and rule fetishism. Do not use it for external legal advice or current-event case evaluation.
