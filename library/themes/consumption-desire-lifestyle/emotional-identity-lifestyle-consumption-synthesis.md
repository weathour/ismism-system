## Claim

The layer supports a cautious diagnosis of emotional and identity consumption: affect, lifestyle, luxury, brand signs, and advertising/marketing engineering shape subjects who must feel, display, choose, and enjoy correctly.

## Boundary

This synthesis does not claim that the corpus literally uses every contemporary phrase such as `情绪消费`, `身份消费`, or `自我品牌化`. It treats them as query-facing diagnostic labels whose backing evidence is adjacent exact wording: `情绪`, `身份`, `品牌`, `广告`, `营销`, `生活方式`, `奢侈品`, and consumer-experience passages.
