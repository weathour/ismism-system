# Commodity Fetishism and Market Enjoyment Synthesis

- status: G013 synthesis draft
- classes: `commodity-fetishism-objectification`, `market-money-exchange-carrier`, `capitalism-production-consumption-bridge`

## Claim

The corpus lets commodity fetishism become a concrete consumption diagnosis: commodity, market, price, money, and consumer experience make social relations appear as object-value and enjoyable choice.

## Evidence route

- Row 34 anchors commodity fetishism: `商品拜物` and `拜物` name the misrecognition structure (`ev:consumption:0034:01`–`03`).
- Row 275 bridges psychoanalysis and capitalism: commodity fetishism is tied to alienation and desire (`ev:consumption:0275:01`, `ev:consumption:0275:02`).
- Row 324 and row 338 show market/price/buying carriers; row 339 adds de-agency through money and market mediation.
- Row 337 shows consumption as capital realization, not a neutral end-user event.

## Diagnostic payoff

A query such as `消费主义`, `商品拜物`, `奢侈品`, or `市场享乐` should return a mechanism: the thing is enjoyed not because it is simply useful, but because commodity form and market carriers organize desire and recognition.
