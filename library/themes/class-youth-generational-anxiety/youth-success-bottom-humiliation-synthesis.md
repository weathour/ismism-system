# Youth, Success Ideology, and Bottom Humiliation Synthesis

- status: G022 closure sub-synthesis
- scope: 青年 / 代际 / 成功学 / 内卷 / 底层羞辱 / 贫富差异 / 青年虚无

## Diagnostic chain

The youth layer is not simply about age. Youth appears as a future-bearing subject: student, graduate, young talent, next generation, or mobilized revolutionary subject. The same future promise can be converted into pressure: succeed, compete, prove merit, avoid falling, and take personal responsibility for structural limits.

```text
youth/future subject → educational or labor transition → success/competition command → failure or blocked future → humiliation, cynicism, or nihilism
```

## Bottom humiliation

The bottom position is often produced through projection: the `底层男青年`, `穷光棍`, `穷屌丝`, `穷人`, `农民工`, or low-income worker becomes a fantasy object through which other groups secure their own fragile identity. G020/G021 therefore kept many excluded controls: `底层` as ontology or `虚无` as metaphysics cannot support this claim. The claim requires social carrier evidence.

## Poverty/wealth and success

`贫富差异`, `收入`, `工资`, `穷人`, and `富人` are not neutral indicators here. They become carriers through which social difference is felt and narrated. `成功学`, `奋斗`, `竞争`, `胜利`, `失败`, and `内卷` then translate those differences into individualized merit or blame.

## Boundary

Do not turn this synthesis into life advice, youth-policy commentary, or a universal psychology of anxiety. It is a row-grounded diagnostic layer for how ISMISM links youth, success, humiliation, and poverty/wealth difference.
