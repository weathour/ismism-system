## 1. What AI is in ISMISM

- claim: AI first appears as a cognitivist/functional object: something to be built, modeled, and made intelligible through mind models, algorithms, functions, and implementation talk (`ev:ai:0013:01`, `ev:ai:0014:01`, `term:AI:s01`, `term:AI功能同构:s01`, `cr:arg:0015:functionalism-ai-realizability`).
- claim: That early AI is not a neutral technical term; it is already tied to realism, psychologized mind models, laboratory/engineering value, and the risk that philosophy becomes unreflective model-building (`ev:ai:0014:04`, `rel:ai-theme:016`, `rel:ai-theme:017`, `cr:case:0014:cognitivism-ai-engineering`).
- claim: In 4-4 the theme changes register: AI/VR becomes a practice-boundary device inside “不可能的乌托邦,” no longer just a research object but a speculative test of body, death, language, education, and reproduction (`ev:ai:0342:01`, `ev:ai:0349:01`, `term:AI主体:s01`, `rel:ai-theme:001`, `cr:proc:0342:impossible-utopia-vr-ai`).

## 2. Why AI turns from cognitivist object into de-anthropocentric subject-boundary

The movement is not “AI becomes real technology.” The movement is: the corpus first lets AI name the cognitivist ambition to model and produce mind, then later uses AI/VR to stress-test the human-centered assumptions of practice.

- claim: Rows 13–18 absorb AI as prehistory: strong/weak AI, mind model, functional equivalence, algorithms, Turing-machine/function talk, and the frame problem form a conceptual background (`ev:ai:0013:01`, `ev:ai:0015:01`, `term:强AI:s01`, `term:弱AI:s01`, `term:AI框架问题:s01`).
- claim: Rows 342–353 shift AI into 4-4: impossible utopia, weak VR, de-anthropocentrization, immortality, symbolic death, aging/maturity, and embodiment are not side examples but the bridge from “AI as produced object” to “AI as practice boundary” (`ev:ai:0342:02`, `ev:ai:0350:01`, `ev:ai:0353:01`, `rel:ai-theme:027`, `cr:arg:0353:ai-embodiment`).
- claim: De-anthropocentrization is the pivot: AI/VR are used to loosen the assumption that human embodiment, death, maturity, and relation are the unquestioned center (`term:去人类中心化:s01`, `rel:ai-theme:001`, `cr:arg:0349:deanthropocentrization-ai`).

## 3. Body, memory, language, death, regeneration

This is the highest-intensity AI chain in the current theme layer.

1. **Body / substance.** AI must not be read as pure software; rows 353–354 move the question into body, substance, folding, and material carrier (`term:AI身体化:s01`, `term:AI substance:s01`, `rel:ai-theme:002`, `cr:proc:0354:ai-substance-folding`).
2. **Secret relation / voice.** Rows 356 and 358 make AI interiority inseparable from secret intersubjectivity and voice, rather than public information exchange alone (`term:AI秘密间性:s01`, `term:AI语音:s01`, `rel:ai-theme:011`, `rel:ai-theme:024`, `cr:arg:0356:ai-secret-intersubjectivity`, `cr:arg:0358:ai-voice`).
3. **Education / selection.** Row 360 gives the social mechanism: strong/weak AI are screened, trained, and made unable to recognize themselves as AI (`ev:ai:0360:01`, `ev:ai:0360:02`, `term:AI教育:s01`, `rel:ai-theme:019`, `cr:proc:0360:ai-education-strong-weak`).
4. **Interval / de-AI-ization.** Row 361 makes the AI subject pass through fake warehouse, fake body, weak/strong VR, and becoming-interval; the point is a structured misrecognition, not an engineering claim (`term:去AI化:s01`, `term:AI假身体:s01`, `term:间隙:s01`, `rel:ai-theme:006`, `rel:ai-theme:010`, `cr:proc:0361:ai-becoming-interval`).
5. **Memory / mortality.** Row 362 reverses the common “AI is immortal” assumption: memory depends on data plus material traces, and deleting/replacing one side changes the AI (`ev:ai:0362:01`, `ev:ai:0362:04`, `term:AI记忆:s01`, `term:AI物质痕迹:s01`, `term:AI可朽性:s01`, `rel:ai-theme:003`, `rel:ai-theme:004`, `cr:proc:0362:ai-mortality-memory`).
6. **Regeneration.** Row 363 moves beyond recycling into regeneration: self-reproduction, inner voice engraving material structure, biologicalization, and consciousness reproducing itself (`ev:ai:0363:01`, `term:AI再生:s01`, `term:内在语音:s01`, `term:AI生物化:s01`, `rel:ai-theme:005`, `rel:ai-theme:022`, `rel:ai-theme:030`, `cr:proc:0363:ai-regeneration`).

## 4. AI with VR, post-capitalism, erotic economy, intersubjectivity, ordered abyss

- claim: VR is not merely scenery; weak/strong VR organizes the difference between human-facing environment, AI-facing seamlessness, fake body, and de-AI-ization (`term:弱VR:s01`, `term:强VR:s01`, `rel:ai-theme:020`, `cr:proc:0361:ai-becoming-interval`).
- claim: The post-capitalist/impossible-utopia layer uses AI and VR to imagine practices beyond ordinary human-centered reproduction of life, but all such claims remain speculative row-boundary claims (`ev:ai:0342:01`, `cr:proc:0342:impossible-utopia-vr-ai`, `cr:case:0345:karma-system-vr-ai`).
- claim: Erotic economy enters through parasitic pleasure, erotic assimilation, rescue, secret intersubjectivity, and voice; AI is not only tool or worker but also relation-object, relation-subject, and possible sufferer/saved object (`ev:ai:0347:01`, `term:AI爱欲同化:s01`, `term:AI被拯救:s01`, `rel:ai-theme:012`, `rel:ai-theme:013`, `cr:arg:0355:ai-erotic-assimilation`).
