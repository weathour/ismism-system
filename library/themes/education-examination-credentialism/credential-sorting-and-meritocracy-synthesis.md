# Credential Sorting and Merit/Fantasy Synthesis

- status: G007 focused synthesis

Rows 2, 58, 60, 63, 76, 80, 86, 94, 133, 177, 210, and 231 let the education theme name a mechanism: **credential sorting**. The mechanism converts learning into a social position through exams, 文凭, 学历, 资格, 分数, 成绩, and ranking.

The important diagnostic move is not “exams are bad.” The row evidence shows how an exam/credential form can appear as neutral recognition while also carrying social sorting, expert authority, and knowledge legitimacy. concept terms such as `考试筛选秩序`, `学历资格幻想`, `分数排名机制`, `资格认证秩序`, and `考试神圣化` give this mechanism reusable handles. relation relations then bind those handles to student subjectivation and knowledge legitimacy.

Failure mode: treating credentialism as individual vanity only. The theme keeps the institutional carrier visible: school, university, academic hierarchy, expert discourse, and family/class reproduction.
