Status: G029 evidence taxonomy. Counts derive from `health-body-risk-society-row-manifest.jsonl`.

## Classes

- `body-embodiment-phenomenology`: body/body性/body化/肉身/sensory or embodied subjectivity evidence.
- `medicine-treatment-health-system`: medical, treatment, doctor, hospital, healing, medicine, or health-system evidence.
- `health-disease-risk`: health, disease, risk, bodily vulnerability, or health/disease contrast evidence.
- `medicalization-normality-pathology`: normal/abnormal, symptom, pathology, psychological/medical naming, or disease-label evidence.
- `epidemic-immunity-public-health`: epidemic, immunity, hygiene/public-health or collective biological-risk evidence.
- `body-governance-discipline`: body plus governance, discipline, production, labor, gender, childhood, power, or control evidence.
- `stigma-vulnerability-disability`: stigma, vulnerability, disability, abnormality, exclusion or shame around bodies/disease.
- `excluded-keyword-only`: boundary-control rows whose body/illness term is too generic or metaphorical for direct health-risk claims.

## Boundary decisions

- No exact G029 clean-text hit was found for `医疗化`, `健康主义`, `身体治理`, `疫情记忆`, or `疾病污名`; those labels must remain downstream and evidence-gated.
- `身体` is often phenomenological or metaphysical; it enters health-body diagnosis only when paired with bodily life, treatment, risk, social discipline, vulnerability, or illness/pathology evidence.
- `病` is broad and often metaphorical; use `疾病`, `症状`, `病理`, `治疗`, `医学`, `医生`, `医院`, `健康`, `风险`, `疫情`, `污名`, or adjacent social mechanism before making a health-risk claim.
