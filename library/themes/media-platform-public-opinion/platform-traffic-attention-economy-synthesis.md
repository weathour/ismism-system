# Platform / Traffic / Attention Economy Synthesis

- status: G016 focused synthesis

The platform side of the layer is anchored by row 21 and its neighboring evidence: live-stream platform, operator, advertising, traffic purchase, short video image, and directed reinforcement. G015 turns this into concept senses such as `平台社会`, `流量`, `短视频`, `直播平台`, `平台运营`, `广告投放`, `买流量`, `定向强化`, `注意力可用度`, `算法加权`, `数据采集`, `信息资本主义`, and `推荐系统`.

The diagnostic point is not that every platform is the same. It is that platform society makes attention operational: attention is made available, steered, counted, purchased, recommended, and folded back into subject formation. This is why the layer must be read together with AI/data absorption, Consumption, Capitalism, and Psychoanalysis-Subjectivity.

Practical use: when analyzing a film, social event, or case, ask whether the scene converts visibility into value, whether the subject enters through a recommendation/traffic apparatus, and whether public feeling is being routed through a platform carrier rather than through durable organization.
