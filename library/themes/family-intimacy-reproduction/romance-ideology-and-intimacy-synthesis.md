# Romance Ideology and Intimacy Synthesis

- status: G010 focused synthesis
- scope: 爱情 / 恋爱 / 亲密关系 / 性关系 / 伴侣 / 爱欲

## Reading

The corpus repeatedly refuses to let “爱情” appear as simple authenticity. In row 55, modern love is raised into a religion-like center; in row 45, sacred love becomes a fantasy of the big Other; in row 85, intimacy is structured by lack rather than perfect complementarity; in row 184, marriage and love become a dialectical social form rather than a private sentiment.

Diagnostic grammar:

`romance promise → lover/partner subject-position → fantasy of completion → big-Other or small-other carrier → impossibility/contradiction → reflective intimacy boundary`.

## Evidence markers

- `ev:family:0055:*` anchors modern romance and love-as-religion.
- `ev:family:0045:*` anchors sacred love, big-Other projection, and the non-identity of true love with completion fantasy.
- `ev:family:0085:*` anchors the psychoanalytic bridge through 性关系 / 伴侣 / 爱欲经济学.
- `ev:family:0184:*` anchors marriage/love as a dialectical social contradiction.

## Use

Use this synthesis when diagnosing romance worship, perfect-partner fantasy, intimate dependence, and the move from love as truth to love as social-symbolic carrier. Do not use it as dating advice, moralism, or current-events commentary.
