# Private Psychologization and Trauma Synthesis

- status: focused draft synthesis
- boundary: no therapy-market, psychiatric, medication, or current-events authority.

## Pattern

Private psychologization is the conversion of a contradiction into an inner problem: self-management, self-blame, repair, adaptation, or therapeutic naming. Trauma and repression mark the remainder that cannot be smoothly absorbed.

This layer therefore treats `心理咨询产业`, `内耗`, and `社会矛盾私人化` as downstream diagnostic labels. They are only licensed when exact corpus evidence shows adjacent mechanisms: pain, symptom, treatment, repression, enjoyment, failure, or social/private contradiction. Without such evidence, these labels remain search routes, not claims.

## Practical reading

When applying the theme, ask: What pressure is being privatized? What symptom returns? What enjoyment or fantasy keeps the subject attached? What social relation disappears when the problem is named as merely psychological?
