# Psychoanalysis-Subjectivity Controlled Taxonomy

This taxonomy is ISMISM-internal, not an external Freud/Lacan/language-theory encyclopedia.

## `psychoanalysis-unconscious-desire`

psychoanalysis, unconscious, dream, desire, repression, sublimation, or anti-pansexualism function.

## `big-other-fantasy-enjoyment`

big Other, fantasy, enjoyment, love, normality, or moral demand organizes subject positions.

## `subjectivity-subjectivation-intersubjectivity`

subject, subjectivity, subjectivation, de-subjectivation, object-position, or intersubjectivity is explicit.

## `symbolic-order-signifier-language`

sign, signifier, signified, symbolic order, semiotic system, or language mediates subject/object.

## `discourse-truth-and-language-game`

discourse, statement, truth-effect, hermeneutics, language use, or language game is the mechanism.

## `ideology-cynicism-normality-diagnosis`

ideology, cynicism, normality, vulgarity, or everyday psychological discourse becomes diagnostic.

## `symptom-repression-perversion-abjection`

symptom, repression, hysteria, perversion, abjection, or erotic economy defines a failure/transition.

## `phenomenology-body-otherness-bridge`

phenomenological subjectivity, body, otherness, or intersubjectivity bridges the theme.

## `capitalism-fetishism-psychoanalysis-bridge`

commodity, fetishism, capital, finance, or desire economy becomes a psychoanalytic bridge.

## `religion-love-big-other-bridge`

religious love, savior, sacred guarantee, anti-idolatry, or big Other bridges desire/fantasy.

## `feminism-sexuality-desire-bridge`

gender, sexuality, misogyny, sexual liberation, love, body/gaze bridge desire/subjectivation.

## `chinese-chan-analyst-discourse-bridge`

Chan/话头 or Chinese-practice materials mediate analyst discourse or language-game practice.

## `practice-analysis-transformation`

diagnosis turns into practice, subject/object reorganization, writing/communication, or historical transformation.

## `cross-theme-context`

useful but non-core signal retained for boundary/control.

## `excluded-keyword-only`

reviewed row whose hits are broad or off-function and do not justify action.
